# Cookie Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how VedicScan, operated by Vedicscan Inc., uses cookies and
similar local-storage technologies on our website (vedicscan.com). It should be read together
with our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

---

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. "Local storage" is a similar browser
technology. We use both to keep you signed in and to make the Service work.

## How we use them

| Type | Purpose | Examples |
|------|---------|----------|
| Strictly necessary | Sign-in, session, and security. The Service cannot function without these. | Authentication token, session identifiers |
| Preferences | Remember your choices (e.g. language, currency). | UI settings |
| Analytics (if enabled) | Understand usage to improve the Service, in aggregate. | Usage metrics |

We currently use primarily strictly necessary storage to keep you logged in (for example, an
authentication token stored in your browser). We do not use cookies for cross-site
behavioural advertising, and we do not sell your information.

## Managing cookies

- You can clear or block cookies and local storage in your browser settings. Note that blocking
  strictly necessary storage will prevent you from signing in.
- Where required by law, we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies.

## Mobile apps

Our mobile apps do not use browser cookies but use on-device storage (e.g. secure token storage)
for equivalent purposes.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time and will post the updated version here.
