> requirements. Publish it at a public URL and link it in your store listings.

personal data at any time.

---

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently delete or irreversibly anonymise:
- your profile (name, email, phone);
- all astrology profiles and birth details (date, time, and place of birth) you created;
- your generated charts, kundalis, and reports;
- your AI chat history;
- your usage records.

**What we may retain:** limited transaction and tax records required by law, and anonymised,
non-identifying analytics. Backups are purged on our normal backup-rotation cycle (no later than
**[30] days**).

---

## How to delete your account

### Option 1 — In the app (recommended)
1. Open VedicScan and go to **Profile → Settings**.
2. Tap **Delete Account**.
3. Confirm. Your data is scheduled for deletion immediately.

### Option 2 — By email
Send a request from your registered email address to **privacy@vedicscan.com** with the subject
**"Delete my account"**. We may ask you to verify your identity. We will complete the deletion
within **30 days** and confirm by email.

---

## Deleting individual data without closing your account

You can delete individual astrology profiles or clear your chat history in the app without
deleting your whole account:
- **Profiles:** Profile screen → select a profile → Delete.
- **Chat history:** Chat screen → history → delete a conversation.
