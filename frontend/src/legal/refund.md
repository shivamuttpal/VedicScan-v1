# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last updated: 9 July 2026**

This Policy explains how cancellations and refunds work for VedicScan subscriptions and
one-time purchases, operated by **Vedicscan Inc.** ("VedicScan", "we", "us"). App-store
purchases are ultimately governed by Apple's and Google's refund rules.

---

## 1. Subscriptions and auto-renewal

- Paid plans are offered on a **monthly** or **annual** basis and **renew automatically** until
  cancelled.
- You can **cancel anytime**. Cancellation stops future renewals; you keep access until the end
  of the current paid period.

### How to cancel
- **iOS (Apple):** Settings → your name → Subscriptions → VedicScan → Cancel.
- **Android (Google Play):** Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions →
  VedicScan → Cancel.
- **Web (Stripe):** open your account settings in the app, or email **contact@vedicscan.com**.

## 2. Refunds

### 2.1 Web / Stripe purchases
- If you are dissatisfied, you may request a refund within **7 days** of a charge by emailing
  **contact@vedicscan.com** with your account email and transaction reference.
- Refunds are granted at our reasonable discretion, typically where the Service was not delivered
  or a technical fault prevented use. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within
  **5 to 10 business days** of approval.
- Amounts already consumed (e.g. a generated report or a used period) may be deducted or make a
  purchase non-refundable.

### 2.2 In-app purchases (Apple App Store / Google Play)
- Purchases made through the App Store or Google Play are subject to **Apple's** and **Google's**
  refund policies, and refunds must be requested through them:
  - **Apple:** reportaproblem.apple.com
  - **Google Play:** play.google.com/store/account → Order history → Request a refund
- We are unable to directly refund app-store purchases but will assist where we can.

## 3. One-time purchases

One-time feature purchases (e.g. a specific report) are generally **non-refundable once the
content has been generated or delivered**, except where required by law or where a technical
fault prevented delivery.

## 4. Free tier and trials

Where a free tier or trial is offered, you will not be charged until it converts to a paid plan.
Cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged.

## 5. Statutory rights

Nothing in this Policy limits any non-waivable refund or cancellation rights you have under the
consumer-protection laws of your country of residence (for example, statutory withdrawal rights
in the EU/UK, or under the Consumer Protection Act in India).

## 6. Contact

For any billing question, email **contact@vedicscan.com** with your account email and the
transaction reference. We aim to respond within **2 to 3 business days**.
